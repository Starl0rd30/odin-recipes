Air Fried Scrambled Eggs

This simple air fryer eggs on the go recipe allows you to have a warm protein-packed mealto kick-start your day while your coffee is brewing.
I like to place 2 fully cooked turkey sausage links in the basket alongside the ramekin for an added boost of energy.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 2 Eggs, beaten
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Lightly spray a ramekin dish with non-stick cooking spray. Pour in beaten eggs.
  2. Set air fryer to 370 degrees F (185 degrees C) and place the ramekin into the air fryer basket.
  3. Air fry for 3 minutes. Stir egg mixture and cook for 3 minutes more. Fluff with a fork and season to taste with salt and pepper. Grab and go.
